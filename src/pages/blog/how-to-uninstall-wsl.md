WSL, for some reason, doesn't directly offer a way to uninstall a distro from the CLI. Luckily the process is quite easy.

1. Press `Win+i`
2. Go to "Apps"
3. Search the name of your distro
4. Click the 3 dots
5. Click "uninstall"
